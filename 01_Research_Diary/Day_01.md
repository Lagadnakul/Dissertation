Today I shifted my dissertation direction from simple comparison of AI coding tools to a more research-oriented topic: "A Self-Reflection-Based Failure Recovery Framework for Agentic AI Coding Systems."

I understood that the research is not about training a new AI model. Instead, it focuses on designing a framework that helps existing AI coding agents detect failures, reflect on the cause of errors, generate fixes, and validate the repaired output.

## Key Learning
The main problem is that AI coding agents often fail during complex software development tasks and require human intervention. A self-reflection-based recovery framework can reduce this dependency by allowing the system to analyze and repair its own mistakes.
