## Paper 11

**Title:**
ExpeL: LLM Agents Are Experiential Learners

### Learned

**Title:**
Reflective Multi-Agent Collaboration based on Large Language Models (COPPER)

### Learned

- Shared reflection reduces computation while improving performance.
