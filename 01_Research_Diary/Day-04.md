Key Learning:

Existing AI coding agents can generate code, navigate repositories, execute commands, and repair software bugs.

However, they still struggle with robust recovery after intermediate failures. Most frameworks rely on repeated execution or fixed repair loops rather than structured self-reflection and adaptive recovery.

This observation further supports the motivation for my dissertation topic:
"A Self-Reflection-Based Failure Recovery Framework for Agentic AI Coding Systems."
