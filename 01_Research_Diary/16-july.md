## What I Learned

- Browser-based agents possess the highest autonomy among current agent architectures.
- Most deployed AI agents depend on foundation models such as GPT, Claude, and Gemini.
- There is a significant lack of transparency regarding safety evaluations and agent-specific testing.
- Model Context Protocol (MCP) is becoming the standard for tool integration.
- Current AI agents still lack standardized reporting for safety, accountability, and evaluation.
